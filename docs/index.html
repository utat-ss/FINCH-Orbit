Interpolation Analysis on Altitude-Azimuth Dataset

Author: Jai Willems
Date: June 2021
A comparison on using different interpolation methods to obtain better position data for analysis. Different results were obtained by directly interpolating Alt-Az data, compared to interpolating ECEF data, then converting to Alt-Az. Interpolation in ECEF is generally better, because of the lower degree of curvature associated with the data, making cubic splines more accurate.

Interpolation Analysis on Encounter Windows

Author: Jai Willems
Date: June 2021
This plot shows the times when valid encounters are detected. By interpolating our data, it was possible to find new windows that were previously skipped over.

Ground Track Shifting

Author: Theodora Girgis
Date: June 2021
This video shows the projection of the spacecraft's orbit onto the ground during each orbit, and highlights when we are in range of the ground station and imaging target. Whenever the spacecraft is within the gray and black regions, it is able to transmit data and image respectively.

Local Pass Arcs

Author: Mingde Yin
Date: June 2021
This video shows the paths taken by the spacecraft across the sky, as seen by a local observer laying down and looking upward. The passes which run closest to the center of the circle reach a higher elevation, whereas those skimming the edges barely make it above the horizon before dipping back down.

A 3D view of the previous video, showing the spacecraft passing above the horizon, above the observation point, then dipping back underneath.
